Early Politics

Harding entered poltics after his success in the journalism industry. He joined the Republican Party and then served in the Ohio state senate. Eventually he worked his way up and became the Govener of Ohio. During his time in politics, Harding gained a reputation for being a good leader. His political beliefs were largely focused on pro-business policies, advocating for limited government intervention in the economy, and the importance of economic growth and stability.
