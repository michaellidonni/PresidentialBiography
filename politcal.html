Politcal Beliefs

President Warren G. Harding was part of the Republican Party. His campaign promised a "return to normalcy" after WW1, he was pro-business, and believed in limited governmment intervention.

At the time WW1 had ust ended and the U.S was facing economic troubles. This influenced his election because people had a desire for everything to be fixed and returned to normal.

Election

The election was in 1920 and harding was running against James M. Cox. The key issues during this election was the League of Nations and the post-war economy. Harding ended up winning by a landslide with 404 electoral votes while James Cox had 127.
