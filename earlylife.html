Family Life

President Warren G. Harding was born on November 2, 1865, in Blooming Grove Ohio. His parents were George Tyson Harding and Phobe Elizabeth Dickerson Harding his father was a Physician and his mother was a Midwife. He also had five sisters and two brothers being the oldest of seven siblings.

Later in his life he got married to Florence Harding on July 8, 1891 but they did not have any kids together. In secret Harding had a affair with a woman named Nan Britton and they had a child named Elizabeth Ann Blaesing.

Education & Career

Warren G. Harding graduated from Ohio Central College in 1882. He graduated with a Bachelor of Science degree. After college he pursued journalism and eventually purchased a newspaper company named "Marion Star" he grew it to be very succesful

His achievements in journalism earned him connections with local political figures, which paved the way for his rise in politics. It also helped him gain knowledge of business which in the long run helped him in his presidency
