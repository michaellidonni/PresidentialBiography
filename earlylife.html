Family Life

Warren G. Harding was born on November 2, 1865, in Blooming Grove, Ohio, his parents were George Tyron Harding and Phoebe Elizabeth Dickerson Harding. They were both doctors his dad was physican and his mom was a midwife. Harding grew up in a household with seven siblings with him being the oldest. Later in his life he married Florence Harding but did not have any childern with her. It was later found out that he had a affair with Nan Britton and had a child named Elizabeth Ann Blaesing.

Education & Career

Harding attended Ohio Central College and graduated in 1882 with a Bachelor of Science degree. After college Harding was interested in journalism so he then moved to Marion, Ohio, and purchased the local newspaper, the "Marion Star". He brought the newspaper success which developed him a reputation for his skills and ability to manage a business. This experience running a business may have helped to shape his political views, such as his pro-business policies and belief in the importance of a free-market economy.
